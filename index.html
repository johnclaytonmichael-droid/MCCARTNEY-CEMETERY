McCartney Cemetery is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to the meticulous preservation of our local heritage. Every stone tells a story; we ensure those stories remain legible for generations to come.

Heritage & Stewardship

Established Ancestry

Preserving the Sacred

The McCartney Cemetery serves as the final resting place for the pioneers and families of Rogersville. As a nonprofit preservation society, we manage the intricate balance between nature and history.


Our work includes the professional restoration of leaning monuments, the removal of invasive biological growth from historical markers, and the maintenance of our century-old perimeter fencing.

Community Rooted

An Outdoor Museum

We view our grounds as a living history museum. Through our 501(c)(3) status, we provide community educational tours and genealogical assistance, ensuring that the names etched in stone are linked to the families living here today.


Your support allows us to maintain a standard of excellence that honors the dignity of those interred here while providing a peaceful sanctuary for their descendants.

Rules of Remembrance

Visitation

The grounds are open to the public from sunrise to sunset. We ask all visitors to stay on paths and treat all monuments with the utmost reverence.

Floral Tributes

To assist in maintenance, only fresh flowers or approved historical plantings are permitted. Artificial arrangements will be removed seasonally.

Data Privacy

Family records and donor information are protected under our non-profit privacy charter. We never share your contact details with external agencies.

Get in Touch

For burial inquiries, genealogical research, or donation questions, please contact our board of directors.
