About Our Non-Profit

McCartney Cemetery is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Our primary objective is the restoration and perpetual care of our ancestral grounds. We believe that a community's history is written in the stone and soil of its resting places.


For decades, we have served the Rogersville area, ensuring that families have a peaceful, beautiful, and dignified environment to honor their loved ones. Our board is comprised of volunteers dedicated to transparency and historical integrity.

Our Sacred Mission

Historical Preservation

Documenting and restoring weathered headstones to preserve Alabama's local genealogy for future generations.

Landscape Stewardship

Maintaining the natural flora and ensuring the grounds remain accessible and aesthetically serene year-round.

Community Education

Providing resources for local historians and families to trace their roots through our meticulously kept records.

501(c)(3) Tax-Deductible Contributions

As a non-profit, we rely entirely on the generosity of our community. Your donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent of the law. Every dollar goes toward lawn maintenance, stone repair, and fence restoration.

Legal & Policies

Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. Information collected via email is used solely for the purpose of communicating cemetery business. We do not sell or share your data with third-party advertisers. All transaction data is handled via secure, encrypted channels.

Terms of Use

Visitors to McCartney Cemetery must adhere to all posted hours. We reserve the right to restrict access for maintenance. Please respect the sanctity of the grounds; no littering or unauthorized alterations to plots are permitted.

501(c)(3) Disclosure

McCartney Cemetery is an Alabama-based non-profit. Financial statements and our IRS determination letter are available upon written request to the Board of Directors.
