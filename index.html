Our History

The McCartney Legacy

A Century of Silence & Beauty

Located in the heart of Rogersville, Alabama, the McCartney Cemetery stands as a silent witness to the generations that shaped our region. As a non-profit organization, we have taken up the mantle of protecting these hallowed grounds from the ravages of time and neglect.


Our work involves the meticulous cleaning of historic markers, the management of centuries-old oak trees, and the digital archiving of burial records to assist families in finding their ancestors.

Community Stewardship

We believe that a community is judged by how it treats its ancestors. McCartney Cemetery is more than a plot of land; it is an outdoor museum of genealogy and art. Our volunteer-led board ensures that every donation is funneled directly into site improvements, from secure fencing to professional monument leveling.

Compliance & Respect

Rules & Policies

Governing Guidelines

Ensuring the longevity of our grounds through strict adherence to historical preservation standards.

Burial Rights & Interments

Plots at McCartney Cemetery are managed under the strict oversight of the Board. No interment may take place without verified documentation and coordination with our administrative team.

Maintenance Standards

To preserve the historical aesthetic, only specific types of monuments are permitted in new sections. The use of chemicals or unauthorized cleaning agents on stones is strictly prohibited as it can damage limestone and marble.

Donor Bill of Rights

As a 501(c)(3), we adhere to the highest ethical standards. Donors have the right to be informed of our mission, the way the organization intends to use donated resources, and our capacity to use donations effectively for their intended purposes.

Privacy & Records

Genealogical records are public; however, personal contact information of surviving family members is kept strictly confidential and is never shared with third-party solicitors.

Contact Information
