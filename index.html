Our Heritage

The McCartney Cemetery is more than just a resting place; it is a vital chronicle of our community’s journey through time. As a 501(c)(3) non-profit, we work tirelessly to ensure that the stories etched in stone are never forgotten.


Founded on the principles of respect and historical stewardship, our organization manages the maintenance, security, and restoration of the grounds. We bridge the gap between the past and the present, providing a serene space for reflection and genealogy.

The Mission

Restoration

We utilize specialized techniques to clean and stabilize aging headstones, ensuring that the names of our ancestors remain legible for centuries to come.

Stewardship

Our team manages the lush landscapes of Rogersville, Alabama, maintaining the flora and natural beauty that surrounds our sacred grounds.

Community

We provide educational resources and assist families in tracing their roots, fostering a deep connection between the current generation and their heritage.

Non-Profit Policies

Visitor Conduct Policy

McCartney Cemetery is a sacred space. Visitors are asked to maintain a respectful volume and adhere to "Leave No Trace" principles. No commercial photography is permitted without prior written consent from the board. Pets must be leashed at all times. The grounds are open from dawn until dusk. Any trespassers after hours will be subject to local law enforcement.

Donation Transparency

As a 501(c)(3) organization, McCartney Cemetery maintains full transparency regarding fund allocation. 95% of all donations go directly to ground maintenance, fence repairs, and headstone restoration. Financial reports are available to the public upon request. We thank you for your support in keeping our history alive.
